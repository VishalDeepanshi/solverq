Features

BuKEDGE an application to manage book publishing and sales

module 1
Author Acquisition / Commissioning

This module helps manage the end to end process of identifying, discussing, negotiating and contracting a new author into the company’s publishing program. Simple and easy to use templates and a seamless work flow keeps the process simple. For a demo please click here to contact us.

  • Managing and updating a contact / prospect list
  • Creation of email templates
  • Seamless flow of a prospect through different stages.
  • Storing of final information of the elements of the book
  • Storage of agreements
  • Reporting.
Select Plan
module 2
Book Production

This module provides a seamless workflow from the receipt of a manuscript from the author through the different stages of production till the point of the book production process being complete and generation of a print ready file as well as a digital output. The module also provides a facility to provide the author access for them to see the journey of their manuscript through the process.

  • Tracks a manuscript through different stages
  • Tracks delays
  • Tracks author communication
  • Vendor management
  • Cost of production
  • Reporting.
Select Plan
module 3
File Storage & Print Management

This module provides a secure storage option of a complete print ready file of a book produced by a publisher. Further it also provides for print management – essentially placing of a print file with a printer through a process and the ability to manage multiple vendors at the same time.

  • Storage of title level print information
  • User controlled file storage
  • Management of print vendors
  • Creation of print work orders
  • Print status at vendor/ title level.
  • Reporting.
Select Plan
module 4
Sales & Distribution

The Sales and Distribution module provides for a publisher or a book seller to manage all their sales and distribution activities covering purchases, sales, inventory, receivables and payables management etc. This is a comprehensive module and is built taking into the account the nuances of the book trade.

  • Customer management
  • Order management
  • Title level sales
  • Sales invoicing
  • Inventory status
  • Receivables management
  • Payables management
  • Cost management
  • Reporting.
Select Plan
module 5
Royalty Management

The Royalty module gives the publisher an automated system for generation of royalty payments to their authors. When integrated with the other modules, the reporting of royalty becomes real-time. The module can also be interfaced with authors for them to get a pre-look at how a book is doing in the market.

  • Author portal
  • Realtime royalty working
  • Title level data
  • Royalty statement
  • Reporting & Analysis
