product brief
"BuKEDGE" is a cloud-based solution

We publishing segment and provides solutions for both publishers and booksellers. This is a modular solution and hence gives a customer the flexibility of using segments of the solution and scaling up as necessary.

BuKEDGE has five modules and the features of each of them are detailed as below.

  • Managing and updating a contact / prospect list
  • Creation of email templates
  • Seamless flow of a prospect through different stages.
  • Storing of final information of the elements of the book
  • Storage of agreements
  • Reporting
  • Tracks a manuscript through different stages
  • Tracks delays
  • Tracks author communication
  • Vendor management
  • Cost of production
  • Reporting
  • Storage of title level print information
  • User controlled file storage
  • Management of print vendors
  • Creation of print work orders
  • Print status at vendor/ title level.
  • Reporting
  • Customer management
  • Order management
  • Title level sales
  • Sales invoicing
  • Inventory status
  • Receivables management
  • Payables management
  • Cost management
  • Reporting
  • Author portal
  • Realtime royalty working
  • Title level data
  • Royalty statement
  • Reporting & Analysis
